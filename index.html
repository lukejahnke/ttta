Weekly Fixtures Competition

Our regular fixtures program runs on Tuesday night and offers competition to suit all standards of player from beginner upwards.

Key Features

  • Play in teams of the same 3 players for the whole season - make new friends and have fun
  • Teams are put into divisions so you will play others of a similar standard
  • Prizes for the best teams and the best individual players each season
  • Only $9 per night
  • Register as a substitute to play occasionally
