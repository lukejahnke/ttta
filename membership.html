Become A Member - Why Join?

Become a member of one of Queensland's premier regional table tennis clubs and discover a new place for games, events, and community. The Toowoomba Table Tennis Association offers several membership options, to allow you to select the membership package that is best for you.

All of our members receive Public Liability Insurance which protects individuals whilst they are participating in table tennis.

The Toowoomba Table Tennis Association is a member of Table Tennis Queensland (TTQ) and through TTQ part of Table Tennis Australia and the International Table Tennis Federation.

Membership Levels

Social Member

This membership level is for the table tennis player who wants to come along to the centre and play in our social groups or to take advantage of members only prices.

Club Member
Intended for the competitive table tennis player who wants to play in club fixtures, inter-club competitions and other external events. Many results of Competitions in this Category will be uploaded to Ratings Central.

Associate Member

Caters for the player who is a member of Table Tennis Queensland through another club, but would still like to play and practice at Toowoomba Table Tennis.

Member Benefits

  • Discounted hire fees
  • Personal Injury Insurance
  • Access to club fixtures
  • Regular TTQ Newsletter
  • Access to club competitions
  • Can be a committee member
