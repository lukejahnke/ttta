Social Play

Toowoomba Table Tennis is hugely popular as a venue for social play at all levels. Doubles play is the preferred format due to heavy demand during these sessions, but singles play is common at the beginnings and ends of sessions when numbers are fewer. Enjoy free tea, coffee and sometimes special treats while chatting with friends between matches.

All standards of player are welcome from complete beginners through to more experienced participants. There is no need to book, so just come along and enjoy a FREE introductory session. New members are always welcome to join at any time.

Those who get hooked are expected to take out a Social Membership after 3 trials to enjoy the extra benefits of Membership such as reduced playing fees and full TTQ insurance cover.
