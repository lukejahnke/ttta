Weekly Fixtures Competition

The Club's weekly Fixtures are played on Tuesday nights and offers competition to suit all standards of player from beginner to State and National representatives.

Key Features

  • Autumn, Winter and Spring seasons!
  • Join as a substitute player at any time!
  • Graded teams for the whole season - make new friends and have fun
  • Multiple divisions so you play others of a similar standard!
  • Competition structure, including Finals!
  • Substitutes are always needed and are a great way to start!

Weekly Handicap Singles

The Club's weekly Handicap Singles night is played on Wednesday nights and is Table Tennis with a twist! Handicap Singles is a friendly social competition and a great way to improve your game!

Key Features

  • Played all year round!
  • Play against players of all abilities!
  • Players receive a points Handicap (don't worry, we'll work one out for you)!
  • Members and guests welcome
  • Games played to 31 points!
  • Handicaps re-calculated weekly!

Tournaments

The annual highlight on the Clubâ€™s calendar is the Toowoomba Open!

This is one of Queensland's longest standing club events and attracts players from across Queensland and interstate. The Open field is usually very strong and likely to include current and past State and National players. The date is subject to Table Tennis Queensland scheduling and is usually released on their website early in the year.

The Club also conducts a Toowoomba Closed event late in the year so all of the Club's Fixtures players can battle it out for bragging rights in their preferred events. It's not one to be missed!

The annual Border Challenge between Brisbane, Lismore, Tamworth and ourselves is a club favourite with selection much sought after.

Club Members are encouraged to enter other club tournaments in addition to State and National competitions. These are for players who are serious about their table tennis and equally serious about having fun. The Club rates this participation highly and limited subsidies may be available for participating players.
