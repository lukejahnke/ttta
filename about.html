Annual General Meeting

Our annual general meeting (AGM) is held in early October each year to elect committee members and inform the members of previous and future activities.

It is an opportunity for the members and stakeholders to receive copies of the club's accounts as well as reviewing fiscal information for the past year and asking any questions regarding the directions the club will take in the future.

If you are interested in nominating for a position, or submitting a Proxy Vote, the relevant forms can be obtained from and returned to the secretary.
